About This Home
Fully Renovated, Move-In Ready in Washington Local

This home has been fully renovated — not just touched up. You get a brand-new kitchen with updated cabinets and stainless steel appliances, new waterproof LVP plus hardwood flooring throughout, and a full unfinished basement with washer/dryer hookups and plenty of room for storage or a workshop. Central A/C and forced-air heat keep things comfortable year-round.
