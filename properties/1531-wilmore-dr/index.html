Location
Where You'll Be

Located in South Toledo's Glendale-Heatherdowns area — a quiet, established residential neighborhood with easy access to everyday essentials. Kroger on Glendale Ave is under 2 miles away, and there's a second Kroger on S. Byrne Rd close by as well.

Swan Creek Preserve Metropark is right in the neighborhood — 441 acres of free, open trails, playgrounds, picnic shelters, and wildlife viewing, open daily from 7am to dark. It's the kind of green space that makes a neighborhood feel like a place worth living.

Getting around is easy from here — Airport Hwy and the I-475/I-80 interchange are both close, putting you within a short drive of downtown Toledo, the University of Toledo Medical Campus, and most South Toledo employers.
