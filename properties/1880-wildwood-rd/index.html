About This Home
Modern Finishes, Flexible Layout, Real Outdoor Space

This updated South Toledo home has a thoughtful layout and finishes that make it feel genuinely well-done — not just patched up. Fresh paint throughout, all LVP flooring, stainless steel appliances, and granite countertops in the kitchen. Two bedrooms sit on the main floor, and a large 3rd-floor loft bedroom adds a private retreat that can double as a home office, sitting area, or play space.

A four-seasons breezeway connects the house to the 1.5-car attached garage and the backyard, where there's a patio and full privacy fencing. A full basement with a washer and dryer included adds storage and flexibility — and the street itself is genuinely quiet and tucked away, which is harder to find than it sounds in South Toledo.
