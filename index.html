3-Step Process

Step 1 – Start With a Quick Prequalification

If a home is available, start with a quick prequalification before you tour or apply. It only takes a couple of minutes and does not run your credit.

Step 2 – Book a Showing If You Qualify

If you meet the basics (about 3x rent in income, decent credit, and clean rental history), you can book a showing right from the listing.

Step 3 – Apply or Get First in Line

If a home you love is available, you'll apply through Zillow. If it's not available right now, contact Neela — there may be an upcoming opening or a new home about to be listed.
