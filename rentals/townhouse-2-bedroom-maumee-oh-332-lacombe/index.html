Neighborhood & Location

About Maumee

Maumee is one of Toledo's most desirable suburbs — walkable, well-maintained, and close to everything. Minutes from Maumee's historic downtown, the Maumee River, and a strong lineup of local restaurants and coffee shops.

Nearby

Kroger and Meijer are within 5 minutes. St. Luke's Hospital is less than 2 miles away. Easy access to I-475 makes this a great location for commuters.

Getting Around

Right off I-475 and US-20 — downtown Toledo is under 15 minutes, Perrysburg is right next door. Toledo Express Airport is a short drive west.
