What We Look For

  • Monthly income of at least 3× the rent
  • Credit score of 600 or higher
  • No prior evictions
  • Clean background check
  • No outstanding balances with previous landlords

Why Prequalify First?

We ask everyone to complete a short prequalification before scheduling a showing. It saves your time and ours — no one takes time off work or drives across town for a home that isn't a realistic fit.
