Common Questions

Is this secure?

Yes. All payments are processed through Stripe, which is PCI-compliant. Your card info is never stored or seen by anyone except Stripe.

What if I owe more or less than what's shown?

Contact Neela before paying. She can send you a custom link for the exact amount you owe — late fees, prorated rent, or anything else.

When is rent due?

Rent is due on the 1st of the month. If you have questions about your due date or grace period, reach out directly.

Will I get a receipt?

Yes — Stripe sends a receipt to your email automatically after every payment.
